HAMAT

HISTORY

1993

Hamat Transportation Was Founded In 1993, Beginning Its Operations With Only Two Trucks Working In The Rub' Al Khali (Empty Quarter) Region.

1995

The Company Succeeded In Securing A Contract With An Oil And Gas Drilling Company In The Same Region, Starting With Ten Trucks. The Contract Included The Supply Of Two Essential Materials — Water And Fuel — Which Formed A Strong Foundation For The Company's Success In Such A Challenging Environment.

The Company Quickly Achieved Remarkable Growth, And With God's Grace, The Number Of Trucks Increased From Two To Seventy Within Just Seven Years.

1999

Hamat Established Its First Official Site In Dammam, Which Included A Truck Yard And A Maintenance Center. The Company Also Signed Several Contracts With Companies In Ras Tanura And Jubail, Which Helped Expand Its Operations.

2003

The Company Had Strengthened Its Presence In The Oil And Gas Sector Through Multiple Contracts With Companies Operating In The Rub' Al Khali Region.

2007

Hamat Launched Its Large-Scale Fleet, Enabling It To Provide Distinguished Services Across The Region.

One Of The Main Factors Behind Hamat's Success Has Been Its Strong Commitment To Safety Regulations, As Well As Its 24/7 Operations And Maintenance Management Teams, Which Ensure Effective Handling Of Challenges And Continuous Operational Excellence.

"The Company Continues To Uphold This Legacy Of Excellence To This Day."
